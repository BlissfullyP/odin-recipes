Churros

Description

  • 1 cup water
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons begetable oil
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 quarts oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Combine water, 3 tablespoons sugar, salt, and 2 tablespoons vegetable oil in a small saucepan and place over medium heat. Bring to a boil and remove from the heat. Stir in flour, stirring until mixture forms a ball.
  2. Heat oil for frying in a deep fryer or deep pot to 375 degrees F.
  3. Transfer dough to a sturdy pastry bag fitted with a medium star tip. Carefully pipe
